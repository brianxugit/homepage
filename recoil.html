Recoil Game

An unused prototype I made during the development of Data Stream. The prototype I presented to the team was developed from the idea of using sound to cause recoil and push the player around. The concept was rejected due to another group using a gun recoil idea the year prior. I returned to the prototype during a break in development as an excercise in animation, shaders, and 2D rendering. I stepped back from the sound idea and designed a firearm instead for simplicity.

Demos

Basic movement and the recoil jump mechanic

Freeze button

Throw a projectile which can be shot. Recoil can be reduced while grounded by crouching. The projectile explodes after a timer which produces a small knockback. Multiple explosions will accumulate; with the freeze mechanic, this can create a large push.
